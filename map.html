🌊 Water safety — READ THIS ▼

You cannot swim the northern half of this trip. August water: Oregon coast 52–59°F, San Francisco ~58°F. Wetsuit-only.
Sneaker waves on the OR/N-CA coast surge 150+ ft up the beach with no warning and have killed 24+ people on the West Coast since 2012. Cold water takes your limb control in seconds.
Rules: never turn your back on the ocean · stay off and away from beach logs · keep well up the sand at Cannon Beach, Boardman & the Redwood beaches. Wading OK, swimming no.
Swimmable water starts in SoCal — Santa Monica/Malibu ~69°F with lifeguards (Day 6).

✈ Flights & the clock ▼

OUT · Sat Aug 8: SAT 7:30 AM → LAX → land SEA 12:27 PM. Car pickup 5 PM — the gap is a walking afternoon downtown via Link light rail.
BACK · Thu Aug 13 (rebook): your original was LAX 6:59 PM Aug 12. ⚠ Check your fare class first — Delta Basic Economy usually can't be changed; Main Cabin has no change fee (fare difference only).
Book the return as late as you can — an 8–9 PM departure buys you the Griffith/Hollywood Sign sunset finale on Day 6.
Daylight: sunrise ~6:10 AM, sunset ~8:30 PM (OR) / ~7:50 PM (LA).

⚠ Monterey Car Week — Aug 7–16 ▼

Your trip sits inside Car Week. Don't book Monterey/Carmel/Big Sur — sold out, $500+/night. You sleep in Salinas (cheap, 20 min inland). Expect heavy Hwy 1 traffic near Monterey.

⚠ Big Sur road control ▼

Hwy 1 is fully open, but Rocky Creek Bridge has 24/7 one-way signal control through Aug 31 — delays up to 15 min. Check the Caltrans QuickMap the morning of Day 5.

🌤 August weather by leg ▼

Seattle: warm & dry, ~78°F.
Oregon Coast: 60–70°F, foggy mornings, ~3% rain. Layers + windbreaker.
Redwoods: damp, foggy, low 60s.
Big Sur: marine layer AM, highs ~69°F. Clears by afternoon.
Pismo → LA: warm & sunny, 78–85°F. Actual beach weather.

🚗 Rental & driving notes ▼

Under-25 Hertz: young-driver surcharge applies — put all three of you on the contract so you can legally rotate.
Fuel: top off above half a tank — the OR coast and Big Sur are sparse. OR ~$4.56/gal, CA ~$5.37.
Cash needed: Fern Canyon ($12) and Pfeiffer Beach ($12) don't take cards.

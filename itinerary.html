Pacific Coast Road Trip — Itinerary

Aug 8–13 · 6 days, 5 nights · Seattle → LA, skipping the Olympic Peninsula. Three of us, Hertz car, cheap lodging. Adding the Aug 13 return removed the overnight-split drive entirely — you sleep in a bed every night — and bought a real water day in SoCal.

Flights
  • Out · Sat Aug 8: SAT 7:30 AM → LAX → land SEA 12:27 PM. Car pickup 5 PM.
  • Back · Thu Aug 13 (needs rebooking): original was LAX 6:59 PM Aug 12.
  • ⚠️ Check your fare class before anything else. Delta Basic Economy generally cannot be changed; Main Cabin has no change fee (fare difference only). At $124/$135 these may well be Basic Economy.
  • Book the latest return you can (8–9 PM) — that's what buys the Griffith/Hollywood Sign finale.

🌊 Water safety — read before you get in anything

You cannot swim the northern half of this trip. August water temps: Oregon coast 52–59°F, San Francisco ~58°F. Wetsuit-only.

Sneaker waves on the Oregon/NorCal coast surge 150+ feet up the beach with no warning and have killed 24+ people on the West Coast since 2012. The cold takes your limb control within seconds, and the waves carry sand/gravel that weighs your clothes down.

Rules for Cannon Beach, Boardman, and the Redwood beaches:

  • Never turn your back on the ocean
  • Stay off and away from beach logs (they roll and pin people)
  • Keep well up the sand — wading is fine, swimming is not

Swimmable water starts in SoCal: Santa Monica/Malibu ~69°F, lifeguarded. That's Day 6, and it's where all three water activities live.

Say this when ordering

  • Vegetarian: “No meat, fish, shellfish, fish sauce, oyster sauce, shrimp paste, animal broth, or lard.” Thai and Mexican kitchens especially need this clarification.
  • Other two: choose only chicken, shrimp, fish, or mutton. Avoid bacon, chorizo, beef stock, pork-based beans, and mixed-meat sauces. The selected budget stops focus on chicken and seafood because no dependable low-cost mutton stop was found directly on this route.
  • If cross-contact matters for allergy or religious reasons—not just preference—call ahead. These are mixed kitchens.

The daily plan

Every primary stop is on or very close to the existing route. Backups prioritize speed so the sightseeing schedule survives.

Day 1 · Seattle lunch

Pike Place Chowder

1530 Post Alley · daily 11 AM–5 PM
$10–20 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Lime & Coconut Chowder, fully vegan and gluten-free: $9.95 small, about $12.95 large; vegan sourdough bread bowl +$7.

🐟 Two chicken/fish diners

Smoked Salmon Chowder or Seafood Bisque. Avoid New England chowder (bacon) and Crab & Oyster chowder (chorizo).

Timing: preorder for pickup around 1:45 PM. Backup for a long line: the less-crowded Pacific Place location, although it changes the walking route.

Day 1 · late Astoria dinner backup

Astoria Co+op deli

2350 Marine Drive · open daily until 9 PM
$7–16 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Vegetarian/vegan hot bar, salad bar, soup, or grab-and-go wrap. Wraps $9.99; burritos $10.99; hot bar $12.99/lb.

🐟 Two chicken/fish diners

Check the day's case for chicken or fish; if none is available, use vegetarian hot-bar food. Selection rotates daily.

Why: the SEA departure makes Astoria closer to 8:30 PM. This is faster and open later than many kitchens.

Day 2 · Cape Kiwanda

Doryland Pizza

33315-A Cape Kiwanda Drive · open daily noon–9 PM
About $12–16 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Share a 14-inch Vegetarian pizza ($26), or order cheese slices ($6 each) when available.

🐔 Two chicken/fish diners

Share a 10-inch BBQ Chicken pizza ($19) or get chicken tenders ($10.50). Do not order Chicken Bacon Artichoke.

Group estimate: about $45 before tax for two pizzas; roughly $55–60 after tax/tip. Across from the beach and faster than a brewpub meal.

Day 3 · Brookings

Khun Thai

925 Chetco Avenue · Mon–Sat 11 AM–3 PM and 4–9 PM; closed Sunday
$15–25 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Tofu/vegetable red, yellow, or green curry (base menu about $15). Explicitly request no fish sauce, oyster sauce, shrimp paste, or animal broth.

🐔 Two chicken/fish diners

Chicken Pad Thai, green curry with chicken, or shrimp dishes. Public menu examples run about $15–25.

Timing: order online before Samuel Boardman, collect around 11:30 AM, and leave Brookings by about 12:10 PM.

Day 4 · Garberville takeout

Eel River Cafe

801 Redwood Drive · daily 7 AM–3 PM
Est. $12–18 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Grilled cheese/mushroom sandwich, vegetarian omelet, pancakes, or meat-free breakfast sandwich. Confirm no bacon or meat gravy.

🐔 Two chicken/fish diners

Grilled chicken sandwich, tuna melt, or fish and chips. Avoid chicken-fried steak—it is beef despite the name.

Timing: place a 7:00 AM carryout order, leave by 7:15, and eat at H. Dana Bower/Vista Point. Do not add a Bay Area sit-down lunch before the 3 PM kayak.

Day 5 · pack in Salinas

El Charrito

122 W Market Street · opens 6 AM Mon–Sat, 7 AM Sunday
Est. $8–14 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Bean-and-cheese or chile relleno burrito. Ask whether beans and rice contain lard or chicken stock; choose whole pinto beans when needed.

🐔 Two chicken/fish diners

Pollo guisado burrito: shredded chicken, tomato, onions, and jalapeños. Avoid every beef and pork filling.

Timing: buy before leaving Salinas and picnic later. Scenic backup: Café Kevah, where vegetarian sandwiches/salads run $17.75–22 and chicken salad is $24.75.

Day 6 · Malibu

Malibu Seafood

25653 Pacific Coast Highway · daily 11 AM–8:30 PM
$11–20 each
🌱 Two vegetarians

Veggie burger with fries, $13.95. Confirm the chipotle aioli is acceptable; plain sides are also available.

🐟 Two chicken/fish diners

Fish or shrimp sandwich $10.95, two fish/shrimp tacos $14.95, chicken sandwich $19.95, or one-piece fish and fries $12.95.

Timing: eat just after the surf lesson. It is counter service, takes no reservations, and sits directly on PCH.
